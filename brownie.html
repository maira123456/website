Brownie


Ingredients

  • 1 ½ tbsp Butter
  • 1 ounce Dark Chocolate 70% Cocoa
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1½ tbsp Room temp Milk
  • 4½ tbsp All purpose flour
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Chocolate Chunks

Direction

  1. In a microwave safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together. Cut the chocolate into small chunks, and leave the butter in a big piece, otherwise the butter will start to fry before the chocolate gets a chance to melt.
  2. Add the sugar, the room temp milk, flour and salt and mix to combine.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a 10 cm x 10 cm (4 inch x4 inch) container lined with parchment paper. Alternatively, you can use a wide mug or large tea cup.
  4. Microwave for anywhere between 1 minute and 1 minute and 15 seconds. Do not over cook!
  5. Once you take the brownie out of the microwave, it will be very soft. You can either enjoy it as is with a spoon, or allow it to cool completely before eating. As it cools it will firm up and develop that iconic fudgy brownie texture.
